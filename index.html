Our Story

The Jonah Otunla Resource Center (JORC) is an initiative of the Francisca and Jonah Otunla Foundation. Established in memory of a beloved husband, father, and leader, the center is dedicated to continuing a legacy of service and impact.

We gather to honor a life well-lived and to inspire the future through learning, leadership, and legacy. Our state-of-the-art facilities provide students of all ages with the resources they need to succeed.
